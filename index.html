Contact Us

If you need to make a return or your product did not come as expected, please do not hesistate to get in contact with a sales representative, we will do our best to get your situation figured out and give you the help you need.

All returned products must have proof of purchase such as receipt or email receipt, to ensure a quick and easy return process. After process is complete a full refund will be issued to your bank account within 24hrs or less.

If the product is damaged or stained in anyway, after it being purchased in store there is a high chance you may not qualify for a full refund. Thank you for understanding.
